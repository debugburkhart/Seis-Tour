Rules

1. Play it where it lies

a. Exceptions: Cart path, tree root, sprinkler head. Can drop 1 club length from where the ball landed, no closer to the hole and not onto the green.

b. Scramble: All players have preferred lie, that is, they can adjust the chosen shot up to a foot in diameter, no closer to the hole and not out of its grass/hazard lie (fairway, rough, sand, fringe, green).

2. Hazard drops

a. Previous Shot: You can always drop from your previous shot and add a stroke. In addition, the following options apply to the circumstances.

b. Designated Drop Area: If there is a designated drop area, drop your ball there and add a stroke.

c. Water/Hazard: Drop on the line of where your ball crossed the water/hazard and add a stroke. If after the first drop you are still unable to get over the water/hazard, drop the ball on the other side of the water/hazard on the line of your shot and add a stroke.

3. No mulligans

4. No gimmies

5. Report accurate virtual results
